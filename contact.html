Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about contacting us and scheduling appointments.

How quickly will I receive a response to my inquiry?

We typically respond to all inquiries within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly at 0348 9006365.

Do I need a referral from my doctor?

Most patients do not need a referral to schedule a consultation with us. However, please check with your insurance provider as some plans may require a referral for coverage purposes.

Is my consultation confidential?

Absolutely. All consultations and communications are completely confidential and protected by HIPAA privacy laws. Your information will never be shared without your explicit consent.

Can I schedule a virtual consultation?

Yes! We offer secure video consultations for initial appointments and follow-ups. Mention your preference for a virtual visit when booking your appointment.
