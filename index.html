Your Treatment Journey

A step-by-step guide through your fertility treatment process with Malkai IVF Center.

Initial Consultation

Meet with our fertility specialist to discuss your medical history, undergo preliminary testing, and develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your unique needs.

Diagnostic Testing

Comprehensive fertility assessment including hormone analysis, ultrasound imaging, semen analysis, and any additional tests needed to identify the root cause of infertility.

Treatment Planning

Review test results and create a customized treatment protocol. Our team will explain your options, success rates, costs, and answer all your questions.

Treatment Cycle

Begin your treatment cycle with close monitoring and support. Our team is available throughout the process to adjust protocols and provide guidance.

Ongoing Support

Pregnancy testing, ongoing monitoring, and continued care throughout early pregnancy. We're with you every step of the way to parenthood.
